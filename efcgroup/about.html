About us

From preparation till completion of your project we are assisting you with the services we provide.

Who we are?

We are a team of professionals whose main area of expertise is assisting contractors in acquiring bank guarantees, providing Joint Venture solutions, mitigating project risks and finding suitable projects. To follow our strategic plan of responding to new dynamic business trends, we decided to expand the variety of services that we provide to add more value to our customers. Due to a change in regulations, we had to restructure our company – “Eurotek İnşaat Sanayi ve Ticaret AŞ”. As a result, from 2020, we operate under the new business name “EFC Project Management Services” that facilitate broader services. Our goal is to produce a system that enhances the productive utility and takes the relationship between financial institutions and the construction industry to the next level.
